An overview

Located in Siem Reap, 1.2 miles from Pub Street, Neat Pearly Angkor Hotel has an outdoor swimming pool and a garden, as well as free WiFi. The property is close to several well-known attractions, a 10-minute walk from Angkor National Museum, 0.6 miles from Lucky Mall Siem Reap and a 13-minute walk from Preah Ang Chek Preah Ang Chom. There's a restaurant. At the hotel, all rooms come with a desk and a flat-screen TV. All rooms include a private bathroom, and certain rooms also offer a terrace. Guest rooms at Neat Pearly Angkor Hotel feature air conditioning and a closet. The daily breakfast offers continental, American or Asian options. Languages spoken at the reception include English and Korean, and guests are invited to request advice on the area when needed. King's Road Angkor is 1.4 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Angkor International Airport, 7 miles from the property.
